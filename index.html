"Hello Musical world"

CREATIVE SOULS COVID SECURE EVENT 2021

A celebration of music and vocals featuring some of the best artists on the globe,let us pull together and come together in this covid secure event to raise money for the world's poorest and help make our world a better place.

2021.04.09(THU) ~ 10(FRI)

@ National Exhibition Centre,Martson Green,Birmingham UK.

Main Program

Lectures
go on a magical journey and discover how music has changed trough the years,while we discuss diverse cultures and their Musical themes.
CC Exhibition
Explore our intriguing exhibition where you can find music instruments of up to 2000 years old.
Forum
share your thoughts and opnion with the community and our well knowledgeable experts.
Karaoke
play and sing the greatest songs ever with our great musicans and fill your heart with joy.
connect
connect with music lovers alike (using are desktops in our top of the art covid secure eniorment) and find new friends to share your music passion with.

Featured artists

Paul Mccartney
English singer, songwriter, musician, and record and film producer
Gained worldwide fame as co-lead vocalist and bassist for the Beatles
Bruce Springsteen
American singer, songwriter, and musician.
Received critical acclaim and attained worldwide fame upon the release of Born to Run in 1975.
Billy Joel
An american musician, singer-songwriter, and composer. Commonly nicknamed the "Piano Man" after his first major hit and signature song of the same name.
Has led a commercially successful career as a solo artist since the 1970s, having released 12 studio albums from 1971 to 1993 as well as one studio album in 2001.
Tina turner
An American-born Swiss singer, songwriter, dancer, and actress. Known as the "Queen of Rock 'n' Roll", she rose to prominence as the frontwoman of The Ike & Tina Turner
Began her recording career with Ike Turner's Kings of Rhythm under the name "Little Ann" on "Boxtop" in 1958
Robbie Williams
An English singer-songwriter and entertainer. He found fame as a member of the pop group Take That from 1990 to 1995, but achieved greater commercial success with his solo career.
Has released seven UK number one singles and eleven out of his twelve studio albums have reached number one in the UK.
Taylor swift
An american singer-songwriter. Her narrative songwriting, which often takes inspiration from her personal life, has received widespread critical plaudits and media coverage
Born in West Reading, Pennsylvania, Swift relocated to Nashville, Tennessee, in 2004 to pursue a career in country music. She broke into the country scene with the release of her eponymous debut studio album in 2006
